General tips from our analysis:

In addition, ApplaudMe found these general tips to increase your probability of success:

  • If possible, write for a middle school audience! The readability of your text is an important factor for achieving success. Although certain topics, such as machine learning, need to use technical terms, an 11-15 year old should be able to read and understand the way to describe those terms.
  • Try to link your post to other sources to increase its visibility.
  • End your article with a call to action—ask the reader to try this new approach or write a comment or discuss the topic with family and friends. Calls to action are used by top articles and invite readers to engage with your content.
  • Post (or re-post) your article on Saturdays. ApplaudMe found that posts that are published on Saturdays (and sometimes Sundays) have a higher probability of success. These articles also face less competition, because typically, writers post 20-30% articles during weekends.
